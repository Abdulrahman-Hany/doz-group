Platform Structure

DOZ Group is designed as a parent platform / holding company that governs a portfolio of operational and technical units. Within this structure, TaxiF operates as the core engine—running real-world mobility operations while feeding scalable infrastructure, partnerships, and market expansion.

The model supports operational engines (ride-hailing, delivery, fleet, B2B mobility) and growth multipliers (Mobility, AI, Data) to accelerate margins, optimize pricing, improve utilization, and increase defensibility as expansion scales.
